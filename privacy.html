Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ruach is built around a simple principle: the tool helps you create undistracted time with God. That principle extends to how we treat your data.

What we collect

Nothing.

Ruach does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts. There are no analytics. There is no third-party tracking. The app does not use advertising identifiers, location data, contacts, or any other personal information.

What stays on your device

Your selected apps, your paired bookmark identifier, and your session state are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's standard UserDefaults and App Group storage. None of this leaves your device. None of it is synced to any cloud service. None of it is accessible to us.

Third-party services

Ruach does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or any other external services that would receive your data.

Children

Ruach is designed for use by anyone, including minors using the app under parental guidance. Because Ruach collects no data of any kind, no special considerations for children's privacy are required.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect the change. Because Ruach does not collect data, any future changes would only be to clarify language, not to expand data collection.

Contact
